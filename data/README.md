# Dataset Codebooks

## Games Data & Games Cleaned Data

"50,000 video game records spanning 47 years of gaming history across 33 platforms, 51 publishers, and 20 genres. Combines regional sales (NA/EU/JP/Other/Global), Metacritic critic and user scores, ESRB ratings, publisher intelligence, and engineered columns not found in existing public datasets." This is according to the Kaggle overview for this dataset. This dataset gets updated annually by Meruva Kodanda Suraj. He is a datasets expert with several badges, datasets, and competitions connected to his Kaggle account. 


- `game_id` : Video game ID
- `title` : Title of the video game
- `platform` : Video game platform
- `platform_type` : The video game platform type
- `platform_maker`: Platform maker/company
- `platform_generation` : The generation of the console
- `genre`: Primary genre
- `year`: Release year (1985-2026)
- `publisher`: Publisher studio
- `developer`: Developer studio
- `published_region`: The region where the video game was published
- `publisher_tier`: The publisher tier of the video game
- `esrb_rating`: The Entertainment Software Rating Board rating of the video game
- `metacritic_score`: The metacritic score; universally acclaimed rating for the video game
- `user_score`: The user rating
- `critic_review_count`: The number of critic reviews for the video game
- `user_review_count`: The number of user reviews for the video game
- `na_sales_million`: The sales in North America by the million
- `eu_sales_million`: The sales in Europe by the million
- `jp_sales_million`: The sales in Japan by the million
- `other_sales_million`: The sales elsewhere by the million
- `global_sales_million`: The global sales of the video game by the million
- `estimated_revenue_million_usd`: The estimated revenue in USD by the million
- `launch_price_usd`: The launch price of the video game in USD
- `is_sequel`: Whether or not the video game is a sequel
- `online_multiplayer`: Whether or not the video game has online multiplayer
- `dlc_released`: Whether or not the video game had a DLC released
- `microtransactions`: Whether or not the video game has microtransactions in it
- `loot_boxes`: Whether or not the video game has loot boxes
- `game_pass_available`: Whether or not the video game is Game Pass available
- `vr_support`: Whether or not the video game supports Virtual Reality
- `goty_nominated`: Whether or not the video game was nominated for Game of the Year
- `goty_won`: Whether or not the video game won the Game of the Year award
- `how_long_to_beat_main_hrs`: How long it took to beat the main story or game in hours
- `how_long_to_beat_completionist_hrs`: How long it took to beat the whole game in hours
